## Script EN
Paint stains are a nightmare â€“ but they're not permanent!
On hard floors: use a plastic scraper for wet paint.
Dry paint? A little rubbing alcohol or acetone on a cloth.
On clothing: hold wet paint under cold water and rub out immediately.
Dry paint on fabric: soak the stain in rubbing alcohol, then wash.
On walls: sponge wet paint away with water.
Dry paint on matte walls: carefully scrape with a spatula, then touch up.
On wood: warm water plus dish soap works for latex paint.
Always test on an inconspicuous spot first!
